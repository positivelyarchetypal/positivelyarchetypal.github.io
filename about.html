Welcome to my Hearth


I offer 100% organic cotton pads in a quiet attempt to comfort and nurture Womankind and relieve Mother Earths dis-ease. I have been gifting, trading and donating pads to women who can't afford cloth for more than 2 decades now. I live a very simple life and this is why I have been able to spread the wealth. If you would like to donate to women in need, I welcome resources, always. Thank you for purchasing from me, I am a real person and am happy to be of service. I enjoy working one on one so if you would like to bypass my etsy shop and the middle person, don t hesitate to email me. I will make sure you transition to cloth and watch with you as your cycle changes for the better and self care becomes an important part of your daily life. Looking forward to stitching for you
