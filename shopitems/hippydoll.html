The Heirloom Hippy Girl Custom made

Description:

I make custom dolls for life affirmation. Either i can choose from my own fabrics with your help or You can choose at least 4 12" x12" at scaps of materials that have influenced your life..baby clothes, an old torn dress that has some fabric in tact, some fabric of your mothers, beads and little icons that have traveled in your life like earrings or pendants, and some thoughts about what kind of personality you would like this doll to have, or some key words so that i can have fun intuitively making up a personality for her with you in mind. I will send a doll back to you within a month. This is a joyful meditation for me and Im thankful for the opportunity to hand-stitch my dreams and your positive thoughts.
Dear Friend, If you are attracted to me, it is because i was made for you. I am here to remind you that beauty always wins. That authenticity is the games best friend. I know that by paying attention to detail, and not spending much time in thought about it, can bring fun, especially if practiced..alot . Yes, I am! impulsive and with discipline gained through experience. I am like water. I always let it flow, washing everything clean. I face fear by going inside. Crying can be a good friend when I am confident of the the results. Pain always brings rewards, I watch for the gifts for they are always there to move moving forward. Love moves me forward the fastest, and again I stress joyfully that practice does make perfect. Enjoy me. I am here to remind you of who you are. The world works. Wisdom walks into the spiral of life with me. Im waiting for you! Arms open wide!
The Heirloom Doll has been created as a mirror for you to see into, an affirmation of the life you are making for yourself. Please pass her through generations allowing her to carry stories through time. If you are interested in your story being told, create a journal to travel with her. For stories woven through time make herstory. she is filled with organic 100% cotton batting- recycled cuttings from another project. mug wort in her head for dreaming.
